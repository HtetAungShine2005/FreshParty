Our Story

2016

Inception of Fresh Party

Fresh Party came to life in 2016, fueled by a passion for transforming ordinary events into extraordinary celebrations. Founded by
Htet Aung Shine, the company set out on a mission to redefine event planning with creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to unparalleled customer experiences.

2017

Stepping Into the Spotlight

In 2017, Fresh Party took its first steps into the limelight, establishing itself as a premier event-planning company. With a growing portfolio of successful themed events and special celebrations, the team's dedication to innovation and client satisfaction became the hallmark of the Fresh Party experience.

2018

Crafting Memorable Moments

2018 marked a year of crafting memorable moments for Fresh Party. The team's expertise in entertainment coordination and themed celebrations reached new heights, earning the company a reputation for excellence. As demand grew, so did our commitment to delivering unparalleled event experiences.

2019

Setting New Standards

In 2019, Fresh Party achieved new milestones, setting higher standards in event planning. The success of the 2019 Conference was pivotal, showcasing our ability to curate seamless, unforgettable experiences. This reinforced our commitment to pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation in the event planning.
